Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Classmode. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

To provide a personalized experience, we collect the following information:

  • Account Data: Name, college email address, and password for authentication.
  • Educational Data: Your college name, batch, and section to synchronize your timetable and attendance.
  • Uploaded Content: Images of timetables provided for OCR (Optical Character Recognition) processing.
  • Device Information: Push notification tokens to send you class reminders and updates.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To manage your user account and verify your college affiliation.
  • To process and digitize your scanned timetables.
  • To provide timely reminders for your scheduled classes.
  • To track subject-wise attendance and generate personalized analytics.

4. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your data is stored securely and encryption is used to protect your password. We only share information with third-party services (like Firebase/AWS) where necessary for app functionality (e.g., Push Notifications, Database Hosting).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your account information at any time directly through the "Profile" section of the app. Deleting your account will permanently remove all associated schedules and attendance data from our servers.

6. Cookies and Tracking

As a mobile application, we use local storage and persistent tokens (session tokens) instead of traditional web cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences.
