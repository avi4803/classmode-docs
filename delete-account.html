Account & Data Deletion Request

The Deletion Process

At Classmode, we respect your right to manage your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated information, you can do so either within the app settings or by following the instructions below.

Method for Requesting Deletion:

To request permanent deletion of your account from outside the app, please email us from your registered college email address:

Email: classmode.service@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request

What Information Will Be Deleted?

Once we receive and verify your request, the following data will be permanently purged from our systems:

  • Personal Profile: Your name, registered email, and avatar.
  • Educational Data: Your college, batch, and section information.
  • Attendance Records: All subject-wise attendance logs and history.
  • Schedule Data: Any custom or scanned timetables associated with your account.
  • Notification Tokens: All device identifiers used for class reminders.

Timeline & Retention

After receiving your email, our team will verify your identity to prevent unauthorized deletions. Once verified:

  • Your account will be deactivated immediately.
  • All user-generated data is permanently deleted from our primary databases within 14 days.
  • We do not retain any user data after the deletion process is complete.
